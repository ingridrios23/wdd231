Purpose of the Site

EcoTours Peru aims to provide users with complete and up-to-date information about eco-tourism options in Peru, including tour descriptions, responsible travel recommendations, online booking, and contact with certified local guides.

User Scenarios

  • What eco-tour options are available near Machu Picchu?
  • How can I book a tour and what are the cancellation policies?

Color Scheme

  • Forest Green (#2a7a4a): Used for headers, buttons, and main links.
  • Light Beige (#f0ead8): Used as the page background color to provide a natural and warm feel.
  • Coral (#e07a5f): Used for accents, links, and important highlights.

Typography

  • Montserrat: Used for all headings (h1, h2, h3) to give a modern and professional look.
  • Open Sans: Used for body text to ensure readability on digital devices.
